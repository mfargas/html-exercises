The legendary Matt Groening is an American cartoonist who created the Simpsons among many other adult cartoons. Groening revolutionized the adult cartoon genre and created iconic characters that have legacies like no other.

You may recognize his creations ranging from Netflix original Disenchantment to his most famous line of work The Simpsons . My personal favorite would be Futurama however I believe it is drastically underrated and in The Simson's shadow.

Here is a timeline of Matt Groening's Accomplishments:

  • 1954: born in Portland, Oregon
  • 1977: Graduated from Evergreen State College
  • 1980: Created Life In Hell, a comic strip
  • 1989: Created the Simpsons
  • 1999: Created Futurama
  • 2003: Futurama was cancelled by Fox
  • 2010: Futurama was reborn for an additional 3 years.
  • 2018: Created Disenchantment
